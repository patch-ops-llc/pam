LiftNet

HubSpot Marketing Hub & Salesforce Integration

Scope of Work

Project Overview

LiftNet is establishing their first comprehensive marketing infrastructure following acquisition by Ascension Private Equity. As a 12-person team with one sales representative, LiftNet needs a streamlined HubSpot Marketing Hub setup integrated with their existing Salesforce CRM to enable automated lead management, event follow-up campaigns, and lifecycle stage tracking. This scope focuses on quick wins through HubSpot-Salesforce integration, automated lead routing, and foundational marketing automation to support immediate event follow-up needs.
